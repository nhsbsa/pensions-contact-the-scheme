There are 2 types of statement you may receive as a member of the NHS Pension Scheme.

These are the:

  • Annual Benefit Statement (ABS)
  • Total Reward Statement (TRS)

Which you receive depends on your circumstances.

Annual Benefit Statement

You may receive an Annual Benefit Statement (ABS) on its own through My NHS Pension. Or as part of a Total Reward Statement through the Electronic Staff Record (ESR).

Your ABS will show what your pension is worth (its current value).

Total Reward Statement

If you are currently working in the NHS and can access ESR you may receive a Total Reward Statement (TRS). A TRS is an overall rewards package.

It includes an Annual Benefit Statement for members of the NHS Pension Scheme as well as information on your employer benefits.
