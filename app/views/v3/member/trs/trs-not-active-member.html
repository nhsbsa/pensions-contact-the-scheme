If you are working in the NHS but are not a member of the NHS Pension Scheme you may get a Total Reward Statement (TRS).

But it will not include an annual benefit statement (ABS).

In this case your TRS will show your:

  • personal information
  • employer benefits, such as Cycle2Work

Find your Total Reward Statement in ESR

To view your Total Reward Statement, sign into ESR and:

  • select 'Dashboard'
  • then 'My Pay and Rewards'
  • then 'My Total Reward Statement'

Your TRS will display on the screen if one is available.
