If you are working for an NHS organisation which does not use the Electronic Staff Record (ESR), you will receive an Annual Benefit Statement (ABS).

Information:

You will not get a Total Reward Statement (TRS). See what type of statement you'll get.

You will also get an ABS only if you are:

  • no longer working for the NHS and not paying in to the NHS Pension Scheme
  • working for direction bodies, approved non-NHS organisations

Accessing your ABS

You can access your ABS at the My NHS Pension portal. If you cannot access the portal, contact us and we can create a manual ABS for you.
