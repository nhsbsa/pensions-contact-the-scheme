If you are working for an NHS organisation which does not use the Electronic Staff Record (ESR), you may receive an Annual Benefit Statement (ABS).

You will not get a Total Reward Statement (TRS).

You may also get an ABS only if you are:

  • no longer working for the NHS and not paying in to the NHS Pension Scheme
  • working for direction bodies, approved non-NHS organisations and smaller NHS organisations, such as GP practices

See what type of statement you'll get.

Accessing your ABS

You can access your ABS at the My NHS Pension portal. Find outhow to access the portal. If you cannot access the portal, contact us and we can create a manual ABS for you.
