If you are working for an NHS organisation which does not use the Electronic Staff Record (ESR), you will receive an Annual Benefit Statement (ABS).

Information:

You will not get a Total Reward Statement (TRS).See what type of statement you'll get.

You will also get an ABS only if you are:

  • no longer working for the NHS
  • not currently contributing to the NHS Pension Scheme
  • working for direction bodies, approved non-NHS organisations, or smaller NHS organisations

How to access your ABS

You can access your ABS at the My NHS Pension Portal. If you don't have access, Contact NHS Pensions to request a statement.
