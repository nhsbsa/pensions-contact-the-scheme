You can view or access your Total Reward Statement (TRS) through your Electronic Staff Record (ESR) if you're:

  • working for the NHS
  • able to access ESR
  • paying into the NHS Pension Scheme, or paid in for at least 2 years in the past

Your TRS will include an annual benefit statement (ABS) which details your pension benefits.

Find your Total Reward Statement in ESR

To view your Total Reward Statement, sign into ESR and:

  • select 'Dashboard'
  • then 'My Pay and Rewards'
  • then 'My Total Reward Statement'

Your TRS will display on the screen if one is available.

Cannot produce your statement?

We can create an ABS for you if TRS shows that one cannot be produced when you sign in. If this happens, contact NHS Pensions to request a manual ABS.
