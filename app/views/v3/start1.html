Use this service to contact NHS Pensions with your pensions queries.

You can use it if you are:

  • a member of the NHS Pension Scheme
  • an employer
  • acting for a member

We can help you with:

  • the NHS Pension Scheme (1995/2008 or 2015)
  • the My NHS Pension Portal
  • a Total Reward Statement (TRS)
  • an Annual Benefit Statement (ABS)
  • anything relating to NHS Pensions

Before you start

Members

We'll ask for your:

  • membership number
  • personal details (name, address, date of birth)

Employers

We'll ask for:

  • your name and contact details
  • your employing authority (EA) code
  • the member’s personal details
  • the member’s membership number

Acting for a member

We'll ask for:

  • your name and contact details
  • the member’s personal details
  • the member’s membership number
