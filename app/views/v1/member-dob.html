Why do we need your date of birth?

When you buy additional pension, you must complete all payments before your State Pension age. This is the same as your normal pension age or 65, if that is later.

We need your date of birth to:

  • make sure you are under the State Pension age and able to buy additional pension
  • calculate how many years until you reach the State Pension age

If your birthday is in the next 6 weeks, you should wait until after your birthday. Then you can do the calculation and send it to your employer.
